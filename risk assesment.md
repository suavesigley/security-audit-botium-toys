# Risk Assessment

## High Risk

No encryption for sensitive data  
No IDS monitoring  
No least privilege access  
No password management system  

## Medium Risk

No separation of duties  
No CCTV monitoring  
No legacy system monitoring  

## Low Risk

Firewall implemented  
Backups configured  
Antivirus installed  
Physical locks in place  

## Risk Impact

Data breach  
Unauthorized access  
Compliance violations  
Operational downtime
